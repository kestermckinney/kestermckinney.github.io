About

Professional profile

I currently work in the Cornerstone Controls ecosystem, where digital solutions, automation, data management, and project management come together to support manufacturers across industries such as life sciences, food and beverage, energy, chemicals, and consumer products.

My background blends formal project leadership with hands-on technical fluency. That combination helps me translate between operations teams, technical contributors, and business stakeholders so projects move from requirements to results with less friction and more clarity.

Outside of my professional work, I build software that reflects the way real project work happens: meetings, action items, data, reporting, follow-up, and the need to keep systems aligned across desktop, mobile, and cloud environments.

Personally, I am motivated by useful tools, steady leadership, and work that helps teams stay organized without adding more noise. I care about practical improvement, thoughtful documentation, and building things that continue to help people after the kickoff meeting is over.
